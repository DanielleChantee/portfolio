Sims Wear Store

Shopify Theme Support

The Challenge

Sims Wear, an apparel and lifestyle brand with a focus on golf and leisure wear, needed ongoing theme support to maintain a seamless customer experience. The store required updates to align with seasonal campaigns, new product launches, and evolving brand guidelines. Existing theme constraints limited flexibility for quick content and layout changes.

The Solution

Provided theme support including custom section updates, layout refinements, and compatibility fixes with third-party apps. Implemented easy-living improvements such as configurable content blocks and clearer schema options for non-technical staff. Addressed performance and accessibility issues to ensure a smooth experience across devices.

The Outcome

A storefront with improved theme support and easier content management. The client can make routine updates without developer involvement. The customer experience remains seamless across product, collection, and campaign pages, with better performance and accessibility.
