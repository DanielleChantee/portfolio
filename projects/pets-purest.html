Pets Purest Store

Shopify Theme Customisation

The Challenge

Pets Purest, a natural pet care brand, needed their Shopify store to align with their brand identity and support their product range. The base theme required customisation to match Figma wireframes, with tailored experiences across product, collection, and informational pages. Third-party apps for reviews, analytics, and conversion needed seamless integration.

The Solution

Adapted a Shopify 2.0 base theme to align with client needs, implementing custom features and styling refinements across product, collection, and informational pages. Translated Figma wireframes into functional Shopify templates and sections with clean, responsive layouts. Integrated key third-party Shopify apps for reviews, analytics, and conversion tools, ensuring compatibility across the theme.

The Outcome

A storefront that reflects Pets Purest's natural, trustworthy brand, with responsive experiences across all major devices. The theme maintains a clean Liquid structure for scalability and ease of content management. App integrations work seamlessly, and the client can manage content without ongoing developer support for routine updates.
