Paper Bag Co Store

Shopify Theme Redesign

The Challenge

Paper Bag Co, a packaging and paper products brand, needed their Shopify store to better showcase their product range with a more dynamic, engaging storefront. The existing layout felt static and did not effectively highlight product categories or seasonal offerings. Responsive behaviour needed improvement for tablet and mobile users.

The Solution

Redesigned key layouts including homepage, collection, and product templates to create a more dynamic storefront. Implemented responsive features such as flexible grid systems, image optimisation, and touch-friendly navigation. Added custom sections for featured collections and promotional blocks to support marketing campaigns. Ensured consistent spacing and typography across breakpoints.

The Outcome

A storefront with redesigned layouts and responsive features that create a dynamic, engaging experience. The store now effectively showcases product categories and supports marketing initiatives. Improved responsive behaviour across devices has led to better engagement and conversion.
