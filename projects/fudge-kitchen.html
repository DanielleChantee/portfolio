Fudge Kitchen Store

Shopify Theme Enhancements

The Challenge

Fudge Kitchen, a premium dessert brand with a strong retail presence, needed their Shopify store to match the quality of their in-store experience. The default theme constrained layout flexibility, and theme-level bugs were affecting performance and display across devices. The marketing team lacked editorial control over dynamic content blocks.

The Solution

Direct code modifications across Liquid sections, templates, and settings to tailor the store's layout and behaviour. Resolved theme-level bugs affecting performance and display across devices. Integrated a third-party content management app to control dynamic content blocks within custom sections, giving the marketing team full editorial flexibility without developer involvement for routine updates.

The Outcome

A smooth, user-friendly experience aligned with the brand's premium positioning. The store now supports flexible layouts and app-integrated content, with improved performance across devices. The marketing team can manage dynamic content independently, reducing dependency on development for day-to-day updates.
