Floor Story Store

Shopify Theme Development

The Challenge

Floor Story, a flooring and interior design brand, needed their Shopify store to present their stylish floor products in a way that reflected the visual quality of their offerings. Standard theme layouts did not support the product imagery and category structure they required. The store needed modular content blocks for flexible editorial control across product and collection pages.

The Solution

Built custom layouts tailored for floor products, with emphasis on large imagery, clear product specifications, and category navigation. Developed modular blocks that allow the client to assemble content sections—hero banners, product grids, and informational blocks—without developer involvement. Implemented schema-driven sections for configurable layouts in the theme editor.

The Outcome

A storefront with custom layouts and modular blocks that effectively showcase Floor Story's product range. The client can manage content and layout variations independently. The design supports the brand's focus on style and quality, with improved presentation across product and collection pages.
