Nutrigums Store

Shopify Theme Development

The Challenge

Nutrigums, a supplement and wellness brand, needed their Shopify store to deliver a smooth, conversion-focused user journey. The existing theme lacked responsive polish and seamless integration with key apps for subscriptions, reviews, and product recommendations. Mobile experience needed significant improvement to support their growing customer base.

The Solution

Implemented responsive design refinements across product, collection, and cart pages to ensure consistent behaviour across devices. Integrated third-party Shopify apps for subscriptions, customer reviews, and recommendation engines, ensuring clean compatibility with the theme. Optimised layout and typography for readability and conversion on mobile, with particular attention to product detail pages and checkout flow.

The Outcome

A storefront that delivers a smooth, app-integrated user journey with responsive design across all devices. The theme supports subscriptions, reviews, and recommendations without layout conflicts. Mobile conversion improved with clearer product presentation and streamlined navigation.
