Adios Plastic Store

Shopify Theme Development

The Challenge

Adios Plastic, a sustainable pet care brand focused on compostable dog bags, needed a custom Shopify implementation that reflected their eco-conscious branding. The store required new layouts, accessible product presentation, and a mobile-first approach.

The Solution

Designed and developed new layouts using Figma, transforming wireframes into live, fully responsive Shopify pages and sections. Modified core theme code, including sections and templates, to support custom UX patterns, eco-conscious branding, and accessible product presentation. Built with a mobile-first approach to ensure clean, intuitive navigation across all devices.

The Outcome

A storefront that aligns with Adios Plastic's sustainability mission, with custom UX patterns and accessible product presentation. The store delivers a clean, intuitive experience across desktop and mobile.
