ESL Shop Store

Shopify Theme Enhancements

The Challenge

ESL, a leading esports organisation, needed their official merchandise store to reflect the energy and scale of their brand. Figma wireframes required accurate translation into responsive Shopify sections and templates, with support for advanced merchandising, reviews, and internationalisation. The existing theme lacked layout flexibility and schema configurability.

The Solution

Transformed Figma wireframes into responsive Shopify sections and templates, ensuring accurate visual translation across devices. Extended store functionality using third-party Shopify apps for merchandising, reviews, and internationalisation. Refactored and updated theme code to support improved layout flexibility, schema configurability, and mobile-first responsiveness.

The Outcome

A storefront that matches ESL's global brand presence, with pixel-perfect implementation across desktop and mobile. The theme now supports flexible layouts, configurable sections for non-developers, and seamless app integration. Internationalisation and merchandising capabilities are in place to support growth across regions.
