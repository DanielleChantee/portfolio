Fablight Store

Shopify Theme Enhancement

The Challenge

Fablight, a lighting and outdoor products brand, needed their Shopify store to have greater theme flexibility and custom functions. The existing theme limited layout options and did not support product presentation requirements such as variant imagery, specification tables, and category-specific layouts. The storefront needed to feel more tailored to their product range.

The Solution

Enhanced theme flexibility through custom Liquid sections and schema-driven configuration options. Built additional functions including improved variant selectors, product specification blocks, and category-aware layout logic. Refined typography and spacing for better product presentation. Ensured compatibility with existing apps and metafield usage.

The Outcome

A storefront with enhanced theme flexibility and custom functions that better support Fablight's product range. The client has greater control over layout and content through the theme editor. Product pages and collection layouts are now tailored to their needs, with improved presentation and usability.
